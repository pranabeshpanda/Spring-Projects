Shipping & Delivery

To make sure any part you order arrives in good shape and in the shortest amount of time possible we have chosen DHL and B post as our main delivery partners.

EXPRESS SHIPMENT: We are able to offer you Express delivery as a service, this is not offered at checkout due to the fact we require the final dimensions of the package to be shipped when your order is complete. If you wish to make use of the Express service please send us an e-mail after you have placed your order so we can provide you with a quote for the expres shipment.

To also ensure that we provide the best rates possible all of the prices you see below are our cost price. The prices we pay are the prices you pay.

Due to shipping restrictions associated with the acid used in batteries we are unable to ship our AMP product to all countries, please see our shipping table for further information.

All prices include VAT where VAT is applicable.
